One Direction's Brit Awards Triumphs

One Direction's journey at the Brit Awards has been nothing short of spectacular, showcasing their immense talent and fan support over the years. Here are some of their notable achievements:

  • 2012

    - Best British Single: "What Makes You Beautiful" (fan-voted)

  • 2013

    - Global Success Award: Performed their Comic Relief charity single, "One Way Or Another (Teenage Kicks)"

  • 2014

    - BRITs Global Success Award
    - British Video: "Best Song Ever"

  • 2015

    - Best British Video: "You & I" (won via live social media vote)

  • 2016

    - Best British Video: "Drag Me Down" Liam and Louis attended,
    - Harry and Niall gave thanks over social media.

  • 2017

    - Best British Video: "History" Liam Payne accepted the award on behalf of the band.
    - Louis Tomlinson participated in promotional segments.

  • 2018

    - Liam Payne and Niall Horan attended separately.
    - Payne performed "For You" with Rita Ora.
    - Payne was nominated for British Single of the Year and British Video of the Year.
    - Celebrate One Direction's incredible achievements with us and relive these unforgettable moments!
